# Summary

Workbech PCB holder, similar size as "Weller ESF-120" which costs ~$50. To make this one it will cost you ~$5, you can easily buy all metal parts in your local DIY store as it's made from largely available components.

# Instructions

##Bill of material:  
* 2x 30cm M8 threaded rod  
* 8x M8 nut  
* 2x M8 large washer  
* 3x M6 large washer  
* 1x M6 washer  
* 1x M6 or M8 spring  
* 1x M6x30 hex bolt  
* 1x M6x90 hex bolt (length depends on your spring length)   
* 2x M6 nut with nylon   
* 1x M4x20 bolt  
* 1x M4 nut  
* 1x M4 washer  
* 4x small pieces of school rubber (to prevent moving on the bench)   
* piece of sponge/foam to hold components with arm   

## To print  
* 1x Frame  
* 1x Frame Notch  
* 2x PCB Holder  
* 4x Nut Holder  
* 2x Nut Dome  
* 1x Shim  
* 1x Knob Handle  
* 1x Arm  
* 1x Arm Sponge  

*If you wish to edit source, [this](http://www.bolts-library.org/en/index.html) library is used for bolts&nuts*
